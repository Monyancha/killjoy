Why is the rental property not listed in the search results?

  1. The most obvious cause is that the property has not been added to the rental property listings.
  2. Follow these steps to add a rental property listing.
  3. It is also possible that the property has been added to the rental property listings, but is undergoing the screening process from our editors.
  4. Another possibility is that the rental property has been added to the rental property listing, but revoked because it did not pass the guidelines in the Fair Review Policy.
  5. As a last resort, try adding the property again, by following the steps outlined in step 2 above.

How long does the screening process take?

It all depends on workload. An average editor can screen between one hundred to two hundred properties in one day. The more properties added to the property reviews and listings, the higher the work load and the longer it takes to complete the screening process. On average, any property listing or review screening should be completed withing 48 Hours, during weekdays, of submission. You will be notified by email if your property review or listing has been approved and published. You will also be notified if your property review or listing did not comply with the guidelines and therefore not published.

Why was I never notified on my property review or listing status?

The most obvious cause is that you completed a rental property review or listing, but did not sign up to join the killjoy.co.za community. Your email software my also have filters in place that filter out the emails that you receive from killjoy.co.za. How do I claim a property review or listing?

You may only claim a rental property listing that has been submitted by a guest. This means that the listing does not belong to a person that has registered to become member of the killjoy.co.za community. Follow these steps to proceed with your claim.

  1. Go to the killjoy.co.za home page
  2. Select the Sign in link from the top right corner in the header of the Home page. Sign in.
  3. Select the Reviews link from below the image with the magnifying glass.
  4. Select the add new listing option from the menu.
  5. Type the City/town, suburb and street address of an existing property into the relevant fields.
  6. Click on the Complete Listing button to continue.
  7. If the rental property is listed, the active listing page will be displayed. If the property is not listed, the create new listing page will be displayed.
  8. Directly above the Street address of the property listing, will be a link "Claim this listing". The link will only be displayed if the listing was submitted by a guest.
  9. Click on the Claim listing link and the Claim listing page will be displayed.
  10. Provide your killjoy.co.za email address and complete the process by clicking the Claim Listing button.

NOTE: No points are allocated towards property listings, only towards property reviews and review views. You can also not delete an active listing within 30-days of claiming the listing.

Why can I not view or respond to my own reviews.?

If you can view your own rental property reviews it means that you can respond to your own reviews and it causes confusion in the way the public views the reviews. It also happens that some members continously click on their own reviews to collect more points. Every time you click on a review it collects points and adds a view to the total views the review receives. It may appear strange that a review with not particular interest attracts so much attention, whereas a reviews that is regarded as interesting, receives far less attention. It is somewhat misleading.

  1. Reivew the amenities tenntants look for in a property.
  2. Follow the steps on Can I leave a comment or respond to a rental property review and leave a resonse to the member and ask them if they can change their review of the property.
  3. Please do not try and add additional reviews for the property in an attempt to up the ratings. All rental property reviews are listed by date and this will cause the impression that this property is constantly being leased.
  4. If none of the steps above solve your problem. Please follow the steps in the Notice and Takedown Procedure and let us know.

What is the difference between a property rental offer listing and a listing for a property review?

A property rental offer listing is a listing for a property that is vacant or becoming vacant and available for letting through the owner, or through a letting agency. With this type of listing, tenants looking for a suitable property for rent can search a property rental offer by City or Town. A list of availbale property rental offers in that city and town will then be displayed. The listings are displayed by date added to the system and individual listing details can be see by clicking on any of the property rental details.

A property review listing is a listing for a specific property by street address, suburb and town. With this listing tenants have the opportunity of sharing and rating their personal experience of living at this property. To view this listing, visitors must search for a property by it's street address. A list of the reviews will then be displayed in the property reviews list. More details of the review information can be seen by clicking on the View Review button below the specific review for the property. We believe this oprtion really helps future tenants.
