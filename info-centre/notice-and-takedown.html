killjoy.co.za: Notice & Takedown Procedure


Depending on the nature of your complaint, please select one of the following notices:


(1) Notice to killjoy.co.za of Defamatory Content

Notes

Statements that are true or that reflect an honest opinion (e.g. negative review) are not usually themselves a basis for defamation. You should carefully consider the facts before you make a claim (and take legal advice if in doubt), as a false claim may expose you to liability.

killjoy.co.za reserves the right to ask for verification of your identity and to seek further information from you to clarify the basis of your claim. Please note that killjoy.co.za may not be able to investigate claims that are anonymous or incorrectly addressed or do not provide sufficient information in the form of the Notice set out below. We will use reasonable efforts to identify defamatory content when notified but cannot be responsible where you have failed to provide all the relevant facts or where these facts cannot be ascertained. Any personal data you provide to us will be used in accordance with our Privacy Policy; please review this before sending personal data to us.

Warning: Any statement you make may be used in court proceedings. Any false, misleading or inaccurate information provided by you may result in civil and criminal liability.

If you wish to notify killjoy.co.za of an allegedly defamatory statement contained on this website please complete the ‘Notice to killjoy.co.za of Defamatory Content’ below and send a signed and dated copy to:

killjoy.co.za

You must also include a postal address, your daytime telephone number and an e-mail address so that killjoy.co.za may respond to the Notice.

Notice to killjoy.co.za of Defamatory Content

I [state your full name] of [state your postal address, daytime telephone number and email address], say as follows:

  1. I refer to the website [please insert the relevant killjoy.co.za website (e.g. www.rentaguide.co.za)] (the “Website”) and now give notice to killjoy.co.za that via its Website it is causing or contributing to the publication of a defamatory statement.

  2. The relevant statement appears on the Website at [please cut and paste the posting in which the relevant statement appears and the address of the relevant web page from the Website where the defamatory words appear].

  4. I submit that these words are defamatory because [please state why you consider these words to be defamatory].

I am [please explain the relationship of the signatory to the Complainant]].

Notes

killjoy.co.za reserves the right to ask for verification of your identity and to seek further information from you to clarify the basis of your claim. Please note that killjoy.co.za may not be able to investigate claims that are anonymous or incorrectly addressed or do not provide sufficient information in the form of Notice set out below. We will use reasonable efforts to identify allegedly infringing content but cannot be responsible where you have failed to provide all the relevant facts or where these facts cannot be ascertained.

Warning: Any statement you make may be used in court proceedings. Any false, misleading or inaccurate information provided by you may result in civil and criminal liability.

If you wish to notify killjoy.co.za of an alleged intellectual property right infringement contained on this website, please complete the ‘Notice to killjoy.co.za of Intellectual Property Rights Infringement’ below and send a signed and dated copy to:

killjoy.co.za
